Pesto Chicken and Vegetables

This is a fire meal that I made all the time in college and is super easy to make.

MMM so yummy mmm. Nice if you want to eat the same thing every day and also want it to taste good.

Ingredients

  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 yellow squash
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms
  • 1 cup green beans
  • 1.5 lbs chicken breast
  • 1/3 cup pesto

Steps

  1. Chop up your bell pepper, zucchini, squash, and onion into nice bite-sized pieces.
  2. Next, add cooking oil to a pan and heat over medium-high. While you wait for it to heat up, cube your chicken breast and add it to your pan once it is very hot.
  3. Cook chicken until opaque, the chicken will continue cooking while we add the vetetables.
  4. Add the green beans and mushrooms and saute for a 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add the onions and bell peppers next. Saute for another 2 minutes.
  6. Add the zucchini and squash last. Saute until they are slighty softened.
  7. Lastly, turn the heat off and add the pesto, mix it around and you should be ready to go :D
